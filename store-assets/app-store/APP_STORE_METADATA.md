## Description (4000 chars max)
Perimenopause is not menopause. It's the years-long transition that comes before — when hormones start fluctuating, cycles become unpredictable, and symptoms appear that doctors too often dismiss or misattribute. Most people go through it without answers, without a diagnosis, and without support.

Clearday is built specifically for perimenopause.

**DAILY SYMPTOM TRACKING**
Log hot flashes, night sweats, mood changes, sleep disruption, brain fog, anxiety, fatigue, joint pain, and more — in under a minute. Track severity with a simple 0–4 scale and build a detailed picture of what's changing over time.

**CYCLE TRACKING**
Perimenopause changes your cycle before it stops it. Log flow, spotting, cycle length, and notes. Clearday surfaces patterns like shortening cycles, skipped periods, and irregular stretches that are hallmarks of the transition — not causes for alarm, but data worth tracking.

**ASK AI — WITHOUT JUDGMENT**
Have real conversations about your symptoms. Too many people hear "it's just stress" or "that's normal for your age." Clearday's AI gives evidence-based, educational answers about perimenopause, HRT, lifestyle strategies, and what to ask your doctor — without stigma or dismissal. Educational, not medical advice.

**PATTERN INSIGHTS**
See your symptom frequency, severity trends, functional impact, and cycle length data in clear, readable views. Spot correlations — does your brain fog peak mid-cycle? Do hot flashes worsen before your period? Clearday makes the invisible visible.

**CLINICIAN SUMMARY**
Generate a plain-language summary of your symptoms, patterns, and questions — ready to share with your doctor. Walk into your next appointment with data, not just feelings. Finally be taken seriously.

**ARTICLES & EDUCATION**
Evidence-based articles on HRT, brain fog, bone health, cardiovascular changes, sleep, and more — written for people in perimenopause, not for medical professionals.

**PRIVATE BY DESIGN**
Your health data is yours. Clearday is built with privacy first — your data is never sold or shared.

## Confiance et sécurité

### Confidentialité de l'app (Privacy — App Store Connect)
Remplis les sections suivantes sous "App Privacy":

**Données collectées et liées à l'utilisateur :**
- Santé et forme physique : symptômes, données de cycle (collectées, liées à l'utilisateur, utilisées pour les fonctionnalités de l'app)

- Données financières
- Historique de navigation
