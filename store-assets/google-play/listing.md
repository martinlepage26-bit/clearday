## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Perimenopause affects every woman — but most go through it confused, dismissed, or unaware of what's happening. Clearday helps you take control.

Track your symptoms daily. See your patterns over time. Walk into your next doctor's appointment with a clear, organized summary of exactly what you've been experiencing — so you can have a real conversation about your health.

WHAT CLEARDAY DOES

- Track symptoms: Log hot flashes, night sweats, mood changes, sleep disruption, brain fog, fatigue, joint pain, and more. Rate severity daily so you can see what's changing.

- Log your cycle: Record periods, bleeding patterns, and flow levels. Flag post-coital or postmenopausal bleeding for clinical attention.

- See your patterns: Visual summaries of symptom frequency, severity trends, and cycle lengths — all descriptive, never diagnostic.

- Learn from evidence: A curated library of perimenopause education articles, reviewed by clinicians and sourced from peer-reviewed research.

- Ask Clearday: An AI-powered educational navigator that answers your perimenopause questions with evidence-based information. Not a doctor — a knowledgeable guide that helps you understand what's happening and what to ask your clinician.

- Prepare for care: Generate a clinician-ready summary of your symptoms, patterns, and questions. Share it as plain text before your appointment. Walk in prepared.

WHAT CLEARDAY IS NOT

Clearday is an educational tool. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace medical care. If you're experiencing symptoms, see your healthcare provider. Clearday helps you have a better conversation when you do.

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

Your health data is encrypted and stored securely. You can delete your account and all data at any time from Settings. Clearday requires explicit consent before collecting any health information.

AVAILABLE IN ENGLISH AND FRENCH

Clearday supports both English and French. Switch languages anytime in Settings.
