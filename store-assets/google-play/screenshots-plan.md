# Clearday — Google Play Screenshots Plan

Google Play requires 2-8 screenshots per device type. Recommended: 5-6 screenshots.

## Screenshot Specifications
- Phone: 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio, min 320px, max 3840px on any side
- Format: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)

## Screenshot Sequence (transformation-first, not feature-first)

### Screenshot 1 — Hero / Value Proposition
**Text overlay:** "Understand your perimenopause. Prepare for your doctor."
**Screen shown:** Dashboard with greeting, streak, and recent symptom pattern card
**Why first:** Establishes the transformation immediately — confusion → clarity

### Screenshot 2 — Symptom Tracking
**Text overlay:** "Track symptoms daily. See what's changing."
**Screen shown:** Log tab with symptom severity pickers filled in (hot flashes, sleep, mood)
**Why:** Core value prop — this is what users do every day

### Screenshot 3 — Patterns & Trends
**Text overlay:** "Your patterns, clearly visualized."
**Screen shown:** Patterns tab with symptom frequency bars and impact trend chart
**Why:** Shows the payoff of tracking — visible patterns over time

### Screenshot 4 — AI Educational Navigator
**Text overlay:** "Ask anything about perimenopause."
**Screen shown:** Ask tab with a conversation showing a question and evidence-based response
**Why:** Differentiator — AI-powered education, not just tracking

### Screenshot 5 — Clinician Summary
**Text overlay:** "Walk into your appointment prepared."
**Screen shown:** Summary tab with a generated clinician summary expanded
**Why:** The transformation endpoint — prepared for medical care

### Screenshot 6 — Education Library
**Text overlay:** "Evidence-based articles reviewed by clinicians."
**Screen shown:** Learn tab with category chips and article cards
**Why:** Builds trust — curated, sourced education
